This release is a big reliability and voice-quality push across ChatGPT, Claude, and Pi.

✨ Highlights
• Read-aloud on ChatGPT is back and more dependable — it now reads the first reply in a new voice chat, and keeps working as ChatGPT reshuffles its layout.
• Your voice controls (the call button and voice menus) reliably appear across ChatGPT, Claude, and Pi — even as they redesign their pages and when you reload a conversation.
• A lighter, faster-loading interface, plus refreshed performance insights with a transcription-latency breakdown.

🛠️ Also improved
• Voice output no longer reads Claude's private "thinking" notes aloud, and the voice-selection greeting plays properly again.
• Voice typing now inserts text right at your cursor.
• The microphone hands off cleanly when you start talking in another tab.
• Clearer messages when voice is off or unavailable, and your TTS usage now shows as credits.

Plus a lot of under-the-hood work — including new automated testing across all three sites — to keep Say, Pi fast and dependable.
