This release is all about your first minutes with Say, Pi.

✨ New
• A proper welcome for new installs — a first-run tab that gets you set up, with a live "try it yourself" mic-test meter so you can see your voice working before your first real conversation.
• Quiet mode, suggested automatically when you tell us you're somewhere a hands-free voice doesn't quite fit.
• Clearer recovery when your microphone access is denied or gets lost after a browser update, so you're never left wondering why voice stopped working.
• A quick nod after your first successful voice turn, showing how much time you just saved.

Plus the usual under-the-hood work to keep Say, Pi fast and dependable.
