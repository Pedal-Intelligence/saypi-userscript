Say, Pi

Experience a Seamless Conversation with Pi

Unlock the full potential of your dialogues with Pi. "Say, Pi" is a voice dictation feature that enhances your interaction with the Pi chatbot by offering you a natural, efficient, and enjoyable way to communicate. Developed to provide a quality interim solution to the limitations of the native "Call Pi" feature, "Say, Pi" offers superior accuracy and multi-language support, all seamlessly integrated into the "Hey Pi" user experience.

Whether you're conversing in your native language or experimenting with a new one, "Say, Pi" captures your speech with impressive precision, transforming your spoken words into text in real time. It's designed to facilitate smooth, back-and-forth conversations with Pi, without the delay of typing.

Join the users who've discovered the convenience and joy of speaking with Pi. Try "Say, Pi" now and elevate your Pi chatbot experience to new heights.

Installation Guide

  1. Install a userscript manager: This could be Tampermonkey, Greasemonkey, or any other manager compatible with your browser. Find it on your extension/add-on store.
  2. Install "Say, Pi" Userscript: Click here to install. Your userscript manager should prompt you for confirmation.
  3. Visit "Hey, Pi": Go to https://heypi.com/. You'll find the "talk" button, enabled by "Say, Pi", on the page.
  4. Accept permissions: You'll be prompted to grant permissions for your microphone and for resource loading. Accept to continue.
  5. Start Speaking with Pi: Click and hold the "talk" button while speaking. Your words will be transcribed and sent to Pi in real-time.

Compatibility

"Say, Pi" is compatible with popular browsers like Chrome and Edge. Ensure you have a userscript manager installed before using it.
